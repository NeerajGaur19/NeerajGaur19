
### Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer or machine to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

## Examples:

* Understanding language
* Recognizing images
* Making decisions
* Learning from data
* Playing games
* Driving cars  

## Simple Idea

Instead of programming every rule manually, AI systems try to learn patterns and make decisions automatically.

## Relationship Between AI, ML, and Deep Learning

Think of them like this:

* AI = Big field
* ML = Subset of AI
* Deep Learning = Subset of ML
